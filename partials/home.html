About Us

We are FRC Team 3200. We are a team based in Centennial, Colorado at Eaglecrest High School (EHS), which is a part of the Cherry Creek School District.

Who Are We

We are Raptacon team 3200!
Robotics offers students who are interested in designing, manufacturing, engineering, and programming a chance to learn and improve these skills as well as apply to them real life scenarios!









Who can join?

Anyone can join! We welcome all skill levels, ranging from those who have never done anything like this before to those who are old pros, so do not worry if this is completely new to you.
The first half of the season will be for learning basic engineering, design, programming, and manufacturing skills. You will be taught by current members as well as mentors.
If you want to join, just drop on by! You can either see Mrs. Kemp, Mr. Kesler, or talk to a current team member for more information:).
We would love to have you join so drop on by and give it a shot!

Competitions

The team will participate in the First Robotics Competition (FRC) in April. The competition assignment will be presented in January.
The competition assignment is what the Robot is built around and could be anything ranging from shooting frisbees (2013 season) to shooting hoops with basketballs (2012 season).
Teams will not go at it alone, the teams form alliances with other teams attending the competition. A typical alliance usually consists of three teams.
Last year Raptacon made it into the semi finals, and it is our hope that we will continue on the the finals in St. Louis this year.

Is there any fee to join?

Yes. There is a $100.00 fee to be part of the team. This includes after school snacks, your t-shirt, competition entrance, and much more.
If we make it into the finals, you will have to pay for travel to St. Louis (around $800.00)
However, we don’t want to turn anyone away. If you cannot pay the fee because of family situations, the fee can be waved. If you have a situation where you cannot pay, talk to Mrs. Kemp about waving the fee.

What are we doing right now?

If you’re interested in figuring out what we are doing right now, we will continuously update the website to keep you in the loop!
We are currently working on building a robot for this year’s game. We are in week three of the build season.
