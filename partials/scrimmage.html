Boxed In

The EHS Fall 2015 Scrimmage

Presenting Boxed In, the third annual Raptacon fall scrimmage. We will be holding the event on December 12 in the cafeteria at Eaglecrest High School. If you are not familiar with our previous events, know that we try to create a contest that will let teams practice designing, building, and driving a robot in a more basic game than the real thing in the spring. Teams are invited to operate their robots in practice mode, and the robots do not interact with each other directly, so there is no need for bumpers.
